# Upsilon-7 Operational Log: July 2137 - Silence

## Overview

This is not a record of events. It is the final, corrupted data scraped from a dying terminal, chronicling the last flickers of human consciousness aboard Upsilon-7. The great horrors of June have passed. The cannibals, the monsters, the ascended have all consumed each other or been claimed by the station. What is left is the aftermath: the profound, crushing weight of absolute solitude in a tomb slick with blood and oil, miles beneath a sun that is now only a myth. This is the slow, crawling, certain death of the last human light in a world that has already forgotten it.

**Note:** Authorship is irrelevant. Time is meaningless. There is only the decay.
