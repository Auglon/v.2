# Upsilon-7 Operational Log Database: April 2137 - The Descent Begins

## Overview

This database contains the operational logs from Upsilon-7 Research Station for April 2137. This month marks the definitive turning point where minor, dismissible anomalies escalate into undeniable, system-wide horrors. The station's descent into chaos begins in earnest as the crew grapples with the first signs of temporal psychosis, spatial distortions, and the unnerving realization that they are no longer in control of their environment, or their own minds.

**Note:** Data corruption becomes more frequent as the quantum core's instability grows.
