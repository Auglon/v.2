# Upsilon-7 Operational Log Database: March 2137 - The Gathering Storm

## Overview

March 2137 marks the final month of relative stability on Upsilon-7. The budding romance between crew members provides a poignant counterpoint to the station's escalating technical problems. The quantum core shows undeniable signs of stress, system glitches become a daily occurrence, and the pressure from Erebus Corp. becomes suffocating. The crew's attempts to maintain normalcy feel increasingly desperate as an unspoken tension settles over the station, poised on the edge of catastrophe.

**Note:** Logs are presented in chronological order.
