# Upsilon-7 Operational Log Database: June 2137 - The Collapse

## Overview

These logs document the terminal, catastrophic collapse of Upsilon-7. In this month, all pretense of order is annihilated. Sanity is a forgotten luxury. The station has become a charnel house, and its remaining inhabitants are no longer researchers, technicians, or security officers. They are predators, prey, or meat. These entries are a record of humanity's final, wretched moments, consumed by paranoia, mutation, and a hunger that transcends the mere need for food. This is hell, rendered in data.

**Note:** Logs are presented in reverse chronological order, descending into the madness. Data corruption is rampant, a mercy for what is not shown.
